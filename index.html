About Tempest Gaming

Tempest Gaming EU was born out the ashes of many other communitys that failed enforcing rules and regulations on members. At Tempest we aim to provide a fun and relaxed enviroment with no harsh rules for gamers across the EU. We play many games competitively and socially.

As a community we find discord the best way to communicate with others in socially and the games we play. Please find the link below.

We are a commuity of gamers based around PC gaming, we find this offers the best enviroment for social and competetive gaming. Many members are long standing within the PC community.

Casual and competetive Gaming
